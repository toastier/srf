The Disability Management System (DMS) is the office at Duke charged withthe responsibility of working with qualified applicants and employees with disabilities in exploring possible coverage and associated necessary and appropriate accommodations for purposes of Section 504 of the Federal Rehabilitation Act of 1973, the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) of 1990 and the ADA Amendments Act of 2008.

After submitting this form, you will be provided for information about how DMS can assist you in the application process.
